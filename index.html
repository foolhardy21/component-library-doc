flex-horizontal-stretch sets justify-content to space-between.

font-size-md gives a font-size of 1.2rem.

flex-vertical-center sets align-items to center with flex-direction as row.

text-capital to set text-transform to capitalize.

padding-md gives a padding of 0.5rem.

font-size-sm sets font-size to 1rem.

letter-space-sm sets letter-spacing to 0.01rem.

alert-basic sets the font and background colors.
